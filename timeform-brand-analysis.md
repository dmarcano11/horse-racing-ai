# Timeform Brand Analysis
## Comprehensive Brand Review - February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Timeform is a **heritage-driven, data-centric** horse racing insights platform founded in 1948. The brand positions itself as the authoritative source for professional-grade racing analysis, emphasizing accuracy, credibility, and mathematical rigor. Their brand communicates trust through longevity (78 years), professional expertise, and a B2B2C model serving both industry insiders and serious punters.

---

## 1. Visual Identity

### Color Palette

Based on website content analysis:

**Primary Colors:**
- **Deep Navy/Dark Blue** - Used for primary branding, headers, and authority
- **White** - Clean backgrounds, high contrast for readability
- **Black** - Typography, strong contrast elements

**Secondary/Accent Colors:**
- **Yellow/Gold** - Highlighting, CTAs, emphasis (visible in "SUBSCRIBE" and promotional elements)
- **Green** - Positive indicators, "go" actions (bet placement)
- **Red** - Alerts, results, important callouts
- **Light Gray** - Backgrounds, cards, subtle divisions

**Brand Feel:** The color scheme communicates **professionalism, trustworthiness, and premium quality**. The dark blue creates gravitas and expertise, while yellow/gold accents suggest value and winning.

### Logo Style

- **Wordmark-based logo** featuring "Timeform" 
- Clean, modern sans-serif typeface
- Professional and straightforward - no playful elements
- Logo variants include full logo and mini logo for mobile
- Emphasis on the name itself (brand equity built over 78 years)

### Typography

- **Sans-serif fonts** throughout (modern, clean, highly readable)
- Clear hierarchy with bold headers
- Dense information displays require excellent readability
- Professional and functional over decorative
- Good contrast ratios for accessibility

### Imagery Style

- **Data-rich visualizations** - race cards, statistics, ratings
- **Horse racing photography** - action shots from actual races
- **Minimal use of stock photography** - authentic racing content
- **Structured data displays** - tables, cards, organized information
- Professional race day imagery vs. lifestyle photography
